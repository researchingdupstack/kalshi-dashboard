🏷️ Kalshi Ticker FAQ

How to read and understand Kalshi instrument tickers

What is a ticker?

Every market on Kalshi has a unique ticker — a structured string that encodes what the market is about, when it expires, and which outcome it represents. Think of it like a stock ticker, but for prediction markets.

Ticker anatomy

A Kalshi ticker is built from up to four segments, separated by hyphens:

KX[SERIES]-[DATE/PERIOD]-[VARIANT]-[OUTCOME]    Series              Time                 Strike/Variant       Outcome

Not all segments are always present. Simple markets may only have two or three parts.

1. Series prefix — KX[SERIES]

All Kalshi tickers start with KX followed by a topic identifier. This tells you what category or subject the market belongs to.

2. Date/period — [DATE]

Encodes when the market applies or expires. Common formats:

FormatMeaningExample

For daily markets (weather, crypto prices), the date often includes an hour: 26MAR3009 = March 30, 2026 at 9:00.

3. Strike/variant — [VARIANT]

Specifies the threshold, strike price, or specific sub-question. Prefixes indicate the type:

PrefixMeaningExample
TThreshold / "above this value"T67899.99 → BTC above $67,899.99
BBetween / bracketB37500 → BTC between bracket at $37,500
GGreater than / at leastG45 → shutdown ≥ 45 days
CCut (Fed rate cut)C25 → 25bps rate cut
HHold (Fed rate hold)H0 → rates held
(number)Numeric threshold6000 → 6,000 measles cases
(text)Named outcomeAUTI → Trump says "Autism"

4. Outcome side — [OUTCOME]

For multi-outcome events (e.g., sports), the last segment identifies which side or team:

Sports game outcomes
KXNBAGAME-26MAR30BOSATL-BOS → Boston wins (Celtics at Hawks)
KXNBAGAME-26MAR30BOSATL-ATL → Atlanta wins (same game, other side)
Championship winners
KXMARMAD-26-CONN → UConn wins March Madness 2026
KXMARMAD-26-MICH → Michigan wins March Madness 2026
Elections / named candidates
KXPRESNOMD-28-JBP → J.B. Pritzker as Dem nominee 2028

Frequently asked questions

What does the KX prefix mean?

KX stands for Kalshi Exchange. Every instrument on the platform starts with this prefix.

Why do some tickers have more segments than others?

Simple yes/no markets (e.g., "Will aliens be confirmed by 2027?") only need a series and time: KXALIENS-27. Markets with multiple outcomes, strike prices, or variants need additional segments.

What's the difference between D, W, M in crypto tickers?

KXBTCD = daily, KXBTCW = weekly, KXBTCM = monthly Bitcoin markets. Same pattern applies to ETH and other crypto tickers.

How do sports game tickers encode the matchup?

The format is KX[LEAGUE]GAME-[DATE][AWAY][HOME]-[SIDE]. For example, KXNBAGAME-26MAR30BOSATL-BOS is an NBA game on Mar 30 with Boston (away) at Atlanta (home), and this contract is for Boston winning.

What are the T, B, G, C, H prefixes on variants?

  • T (Threshold): Price/value above this level
  • B (Bracket/Between): Price in a range starting at this level
  • G (Greater/at least): At least this many (days, count, etc.)
  • C (Cut): Fed rate cut by this many basis points
  • H (Hold): Fed holds rates unchanged

Can I search by ticker on the dashboard?

Yes! The search box on the main dashboard searches across market titles, event names, categories, and tickers. Try searching for KXBTC to find all Bitcoin markets, or KXFED for Fed-related markets.

Are tickers stable?

Yes. Once a market is created, its ticker never changes. You can use tickers to track specific markets over time or reference them in the Kalshi API.
